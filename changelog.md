# Changelog

## Fixes

These fixes apply to Minecraft 1.18.2, 1.19.2, 1.20.1, 1.21.1, 26.1.2, and 26.2.

- Fixed cushions breaking or disappearing when placed next to blocks, especially at negative world coordinates.
- Fixed inconsistent cushion placement on snow layers, slabs, and other partial blocks.
- Fixed nearby non-solid blocks, such as signs, being handled incorrectly by cushion placement checks.
- Fixed seated players' bodies and legs snapping to the four cardinal directions. Players can now turn naturally while sitting.

## Compatibility

- Lowered the minimum NeoForge version for Minecraft 1.21.1 from 21.1.235 to 21.1.228.
